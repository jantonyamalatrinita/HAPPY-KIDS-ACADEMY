HAPPY KIDS ACADEMY

As parents, you want nothing but the best for your child. Choosing the first school is the most important decision. Early experiences in the school environment have a lasting impact on children’s future learning.

Happy Kids Academy was established in 2017. It was founded by Ms. C. Catherine, a passionate and experienced educational entrepreneur, to provide every child with a progressive preschool education that focuses on the holistic development of young people. We recognize and value each child’s individuality and encourage them to discover their passions and interests at a young age. We recognize that children’s first experiences in the school environment will have a lasting impact on their later learning.

It helps to understand the learning process holistically, the aura of the place and the quality of teaching methods and teachers. At Happy Kids Academy, we welcome all parents to visit us, take a look and experience the fun learning environment we offer to all children on our campus.

KEY FEATURES

  • We have tie up with all the leading Schools in our neighborhood.
  • We follow The Macmillan Education for our Curriculum
  • Individual care for a holistic development.
  • School Assembly is conducted on a daily basis
  • Parents and Teachers Meetings (PTM’s) are conducted on a regular basis.
  • Photos and Videos are shared regularly with Parents on their Children’s progress.
